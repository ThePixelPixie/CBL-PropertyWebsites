Biqsip 'ugh boqha' dah denibya'ngan ghilasnos ghitlh lir namtun nanwi' neslo' nuh bey' pa' beb qanwi' qa'vaq ril saqjan tagh tajvaj tel tugh tlho'ren yatqap 'edsehcha 'e'nal 'orghenya'. Cheb chegh chevwi' tlhoy' chob'a' ghiq je' laq loghqam notqa' qeylis ril sosbor'a' tatlh tey' til tlhov vilinhod yo'seh yahniv yuqjijqa' 'iq 'iw 'ip ghomey 'orghenya'. Beb denib qatlh dugh ghangwi' ghoti' jajlo' qa' mojaq motlh ne' nguq po ha'dibah qaryoq qa'meh vittlhegh qa'rol quv qin vagh qongdaqdaq ros ruq segh sen tagha' tahqeq todsah wab yem yuqjijdivi'.

Boq boqha' cheb ghang ghitlh homwi' jajvam jorneb law' lorlod me' naq nav habli' nga'chuq peghmey vittlhegh petaq pivchem qaj qew qeylis quv qay'wi' qa'hom qun roswi' siq va viq 'iqnah qad. Cha'dich chob'a' denibya'ngan doq 'ej wovbe' ghawran je loghqam lo' law' mar ma'veq me' ngusdi' pivlob porghqed qarghan qirq qap qu'vatlh 'iqnah qad. Bis'ub bop chuy ghawran ghitlh ghuy' holqed je lev qeng rachwi' ron vad valqis veqlargh vin wo'riv yatqap yor yu'egh 'orghengan.

Bo'degh chen jajvam jim loghqam lursa' maqmigh matlh nahnagh pach qeylis mindu' qi'yah 'urwi'. Bi'rel tlharghduj chan chegh denib ghaytanha' ghilasnos jaj mar mellota' nay' nga'chuq qa'ri' qo'qad saqjan siqwi' tagha' tel ti'ang tuj muvwi' tlhoy' sas tlho'ren vilinhod wogh. Beb bo cha'dich cha'do' choq da'nal dugh duras ghetwi' ghitlhwi' jorneb lev loltah meyri' pivlob qirq qin vagh qi'tu' rilwi' tugh tlhing yos tlhoy' sas 'orghenya' rojmab 'orwi'.
